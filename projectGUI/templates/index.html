SKIPPD Dataset

The SKIPPD dataset from Stanford University provides 3 years (2017-2019) of 1-min interval sky images (64Ã—64) captured by a fisheye camera and paired PV output data from a 30-kW rooftop array. Designed for deep learning-based solar forecasting, it also supports research in cloud classification, segmentation, and motion prediction. Raw high-resolution data is available.
